# **How to Buy a TV in 2026 According to Your Needs**

<br />

## 1. **Decide Your Primary Use**
Are you buying for movies, sports, gaming, or general TV shows?

<br />

## 2. **Choose the Right Screen Size**
Pick a size that fits your viewing distance and room space.

<br />

## 3. **Select the Display Type**
LED, QLED, OLED, or Mini-LED — each has different contrast and color.

<br />

## 4. **Check Resolution**
4K is now standard; 8K may be available but isn’t necessary for most.

<br />

## 5. **Refresh Rate**
Higher refresh rates (120 Hz+) are great for gaming and sports.

<br />

## 6. **HDR Support**
Look for HDR10+, Dolby Vision, or HLG for better contrast and color.

<br />

## 7. **Smart TV Platform**
Android TV, WebOS, Tizen — choose based on app availability and ease of use.

<br />

## 8. **Gaming Features**
Low input lag, ALLM, VRR, and HDMI 2.1 are important for next-gen consoles.

<br />

## 9. **Audio Quality**
Built-in soundbars or support for eARC and external sound systems.

<br />

## 10. **Connectivity**
Multiple HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi, and Ethernet ports for all devices.

<br />

## 11. **Energy Efficiency**
Check energy ratings and consumption if usage is heavy.

<br />

## 12. **Design & Bezel**
Slim bezels and sleek stands complement modern interiors.

<br />

## 13. **Viewing Angles**
Good viewing angles matter if you sit off-center.

<br />

## 14. **Remote & Voice Control**
Ease of use with smart remotes and voice assistants.

<br />

## 15. **Warranty & After-Sales Service**
Longer warranty and good service network reduce future hassles.

<br />

## 16. **Price vs Features**
Compare across brands and models within your budget.

<br />

## 17. **Brand Reliability**
Check brand reviews and long-term performance.

<br />

## 18. **Wall Mounting Support**
Check VESA mount compatibility and included hardware.

<br />

## 19. **Demo Before Buying**
If possible, view picture quality in store under real conditions.

<br />

## 20. **Final Comparison**
Compare picture, sound, smart features, and price before choosing.
